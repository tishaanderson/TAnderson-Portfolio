<- A bit about me ->

I know nothing...I mean, NOTHING!!... about coding. In 2013, I graduated from the University of North Texas with a Bachelor of Science in Applied Behavior Analysis. Unfortunately, I never had an opportunity to use my degree. Instead, I worked the next 6 years bartending at various restaurants. It was pretty kush, however, nearing my 30s, I realized having steady health insurance might be nice.

In February of 2020, I started working in a custom print shop just in-time for the covid shut downs to begin. I had health insurance immediately with my new job. I kept bartending on the weekends until the restraunts shut down. I got SO lucky!!! I made my change EXACTLY when I needed.

THANK YOU, UNIVERSE!!

While working there, I met my best friend and love of my life. A few months after that, I got my first fur-baby, Smokey (aka Smokey Bear). A year later, I bought my first [tiny] house, on an acre, in the middle of nowhere, texas. We had to quickly fill that space with two sweet girls- Wendy and Moody.

I absolutely LOVE where I am in life right now. The only thing missing is the ability to be home more. I want the freedom to work wherever I'd like and, of course, have the financial freedom that can come with it. So, here I am! I believe coding can be that solution for me. It just makes sense. My hopes are this can be the cherry on top for reaching my dreams in life.

With all that said-- don't judge too hard!
